Alappuzha

Officially called Alappuzha, Alleppey is the picture-perfect place known for its beautiful backwaters and the houseboats offering overnight stays. The coastline of Alleppey offers some of the best beaches in Kerala with water sports during the dry season.

PLACES YOU SHOULD VISIT

Krishnapuram Palace

One of the top places to visit in Alleppey, the Krishnapuram Palace is indeed a sight to behold. It is the best place to visit for those who want to gain an insight into the culture, times and life of the people of Kerala. When you spend a day here, you will feel rejuvenated from within your mind, body and soul. Krishnapuram Palace is a museum and is one of the palaces in South India. Its history can be traced back to the 18th Centurym

Kuttanad Backwaters

Alleppey is known for the presence of beautiful backwaters that add a romantic feel to every place. Kuttanad Backwaters are full of shimmering waterways, rivers, canals, lakes and creeks. The beautiful region of Kuttanad is covered by the hills and sea which offer tourists wonderful vistas. It is considered the backwaters paradise of the country and is one of the top places to visit in Alleppey.
