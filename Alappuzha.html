Alleppey is a beautiful destination in Kerala and tourists can never have enough of it. It is known for the excellent beaches and the backwaters which leave tourists feeling calm and peaceful in no time. If you are planning to visit Alleppey, remember that there are a number of places to visit and numerous things to do here. You will be mesmerised by the stunning beauty of Alleppey the minute you touch down in the city. A favourite amongst tourists, this destination will never fail to impress you in terms of natural beauty and picturesque locations. There are many places to visit here, right from the sight of the sea to the backwaters and the temples. There is a reason why this place is known as the ‘Venice of the East’. Alleppey is located at about 53 km from Cochin and is easy to reach by road. It is celebrated for the serene backwards which connect Cochin to Kumarakom. Apart from the must-visit backwaters,

1

Krishnapuram Palace

One of the top places to visit in Alleppey, the Krishnapuram Palace is indeed a sight to behold. It is the best place to visit for those who want to gain an insight into the culture, times and life of the people of Kerala. When you spend a day here, you will feel rejuvenated from within your mind, body and soul. Krishnapuram Palace is a museum and is one of the palaces in South India. Its history can be traced back to the 18th Century.

2

Kuttanad Backwaters

Alleppey is known for the presence of beautiful backwaters that add a romantic feel to every place. Kuttanad Backwaters are full of shimmering waterways, rivers, canals, lakes and creeks. The beautiful region of Kuttanad is covered by the hills and sea which offer tourists wonderful vistas. It is considered the backwaters paradise of the country and is one of the top places to visit in Alleppey. It has geographical peculiarities in addition to the paddy fields that make it interesting and worth a visit. There are stretches of paddy fields which are beautifully interlaced with the backwaters. Once you reach the backwaters, be ready for incredible sights.

3

Marari Beach

There is nothing like Marari Beach anywhere in Kerala. If you want to cut off from the world and unwind completely, Marari Beach is one of the best places to visit in Alleppey. There is a small village which is located very close to Marari Beach, and this is what makes it attractive to tourists. The village attracts people from across the globe and helps them understand the culture and life of the state of Kerala. It is known for the beautiful glistening sand and crystal clear waters. You can view the best sunset and sunrise from this beach here. It is quite popular amongst couples, and with good reason.
